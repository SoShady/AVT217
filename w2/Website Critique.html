Website Critique

Good Websites

Yehuda Devir Personal Website

The homepage is direct and immediately shows the portfolio. At the top, there’s a clean and clear way to navigate the site.

Brianne Drouhard Portfolio Website

It’s a bit smaller than the previous website, but it’s still clean and clear. The animated background is a nice touch and the simplified design allows it to run smoothly and not lag.

Bad Websites

Suzanne Collins Personal Website

The formatting of this website is bland. The colors are at least pleasing to look at, but the spacing is very inconsistent on the homepage.

Possible Shopping Website

This website has no organization. There’s too many things going on and I can’t figure out easily how to navigate it or what it’s about.
